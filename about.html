About Palette Pulse

Our Story

Palette Pulse was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between talented artists and art lovers who appreciate unique, meaningful pieces. Founded in 2025, we've created a platform where creativity meets accessibility.

Our journey began when we realized that many incredible artists struggled to reach audiences who would truly appreciate their work, while art enthusiasts often found it challenging to discover authentic, original pieces that resonated with their personal style.

Today, Palette Pulse curates a diverse collection of original paintings, limited edition prints, and unique artworks from emerging and established artists across India and beyond. Each piece in our collection tells a story, captures an emotion, or offers a new perspective on the world around us.

Our Mission

To democratize art by making original, high-quality artworks accessible to everyone, while providing artists with a platform to showcase their talent and reach wider audiences.

Our Vision

To become the leading destination for art lovers seeking unique, meaningful pieces that transform spaces and inspire lives.
