About the Artist

Dnyaneshwar's unique mastery is in painting detailed carving work on canvas. Brought up in a highly cultured background, his paintings are heavily influenced by Indian culture, tradition and religion and the same is depicted in his art. Mane prefers to chose the simple life and hence lives in a quiet town of Maharasthra from where he produces his lovely paintings.

All his paintings are a work of imagination wherein he combines the various temple cultures of India into one painting. His detailed work showcases the rich heritage and spiritual essence of Indian temple architecture, creating visually stunning compositions that resonate with art enthusiasts and cultural connoisseurs alike.
