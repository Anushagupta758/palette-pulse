About the Artist

Jyotsna Sonavane is a contemporary artist known for her vibrant acrylic paintings that explore modern themes and artistic expressions. Her work showcases a unique blend of traditional techniques with contemporary sensibilities, creating visually striking compositions that resonate with art enthusiasts.

With a focus on acrylic medium, Jyotsna creates dynamic artworks that capture the essence of modern life while maintaining artistic integrity. Her paintings are characterized by bold colors, expressive brushwork, and thoughtful compositions that invite viewers to explore deeper meanings.
